How it Works

From raw feedback to deep understanding in three simple steps.

1

Data Ingestion

Upload your program feedback CSV or paste individual comments into our dashboard. We support bulk processing for thousands of entries at once.

2

AI Neural Processing

The text is tokenized and passed through our fine-tuned DistilBERT transformer. The model analyzes the context, semantics, and sentiment of every word to determine the overall tone.

3

Insight Generation

Results are presented in a comprehensive dashboard with sentiment trends, confidence intervals, and historical tracking to help you monitor program health over time.
